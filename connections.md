## Connections

### Radical Open Access Collective (ROAC)

The Radical Open Access Collective is a scholar-led, horizontal alliance of presses, journals, and open access projects, with a focus on the humanities and social sciences. ROAC promotes community-governed publishing, mutual support across initiatives, and practical resources for groups that want to build alternatives to commercial academic publishing.

- About ROAC: [https://radicaloa.org/about/](https://radicaloa.org/about/)

### Free Journal Network

The Free Journal Network is a community of scholar-led, fee-free journals committed to non-profit, open access publishing and shared standards for editorial quality and transparency.

- Free Journal Network: [https://freejournals.org/](https://freejournals.org/)

### Slow Science

Slow Science is an initiative that argues for research cultures with more time for thinking, reading, collaboration, and careful knowledge production, instead of acceleration driven by metrics.

- Slow Science: [https://www.slow-science.com/](https://www.slow-science.com/)

### German Postdoc Network (InfoHub)

The German Postdoc Network InfoHub collects resources, guidance, and community information for postdoctoral researchers, including working conditions, career development, and support structures in Germany.

- German Postdoc Network InfoHub: [https://germanpostdoc.net/#infohub](https://germanpostdoc.net/#infohub)
